About me

In 2003, I was born in Quezon City. I am a second year college student in Universidad de Dagupan aiming to become a Web Developer. I've been spending my spare time doing self study, research and slowly but surely; learning to do some projects from scratch. I'm looking forward to showcase those projects I've been trying do do here on my portfolio someday.

The very first interaction between me and a computer is back when I was in grade 3 because Microsoft Paint caught my attention. I also used to do some school projects in a computer shop and by that time, I knew that I really need to pursue a Computer related course.

In my Junior High School (JHS) days, I studied Information Communication Technology (ICT) when I was in grade 9 & 10.

I graduated Senior High School (SHS) in Pangasinan National High School (PNHS) taking Technical-Vocational-Livehood (TVL) Track and Information Communication Technology (ICT) Strand.

I am currently a second year college student, pursuing Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) in Universidad de Dagupan (UdD).
